01 · 新風引入FRESH AIR INTAKE

潔淨新風Clean fresh air

從室外引入新鮮空氣，經 HEPA 高效濾網及活性碳層過濾 PM2.5、花粉、廢氣，每小時換氣 1-3 次以上，徹底解決室內 CO₂ 及異味問題。 Outdoor air drawn in through HEPA + activated carbon filtration removes PM2.5, pollen, and pollutants. 1-3 ACH ventilation rate solves CO₂ build-up and stale air at the source.

02 · 智能感應SMART SENSING

五合一空氣探測5-in-1 air detector

內置五合一空氣探測器，自動感應 PM2.5、CO₂、VOC、溫度、濕度。GEC V 系列共設四個模式：抽濕、新風、混合、智能 — 機器自動因應室內空氣質素切換。 A built-in 5-in-1 sensor reads PM2.5, CO₂, VOCs, temperature, and humidity. The GEC V series runs four modes — dehumidify, fresh-air, mixed, and intelligent — switching automatically based on real-time air quality.

03 · 抽濕除菌DEHUMIDIFY & STERILISE

恆定 50-60%Hold 50-60% RH

精準控制濕度於 50-60%，杜絕霉菌、塵蟎及木製家具受潮。UV-C 紫外線殺菌燈即時消滅蒸發器表面細菌與微生物。 Precision humidity control holds 50-60% RH — the sweet spot that stops mould, dust mites, and timber damage. A UV-C lamp neutralises bacteria and microbes on the evaporator surface in real time.

常見問題Questions

一切你想知道的。 Everything you might want to know.

什麼是「新風淨化天花式抽濕機」？What is a "fresh-air ceiling dehumidifier"?

傳統抽濕機只在室內循環空氣 — 解決濕度，但不能解決室內 CO₂ 累積、PM2.5、烹飪異味等問題。DBA GEC V 系列新風淨化天花式抽濕機具備獨立新風閥及回風閥，從室外引入經 HEPA 過濾的新鮮空氣，配合內置五合一空氣探測器自動偵測 PM2.5、CO₂、VOC、溫度與濕度，並於四個模式 (抽濕 / 新風 / 混合 / 智能) 之間自動切換。一台天花機，新風 × 抽濕 × 淨化 × 智能感應。 A regular dehumidifier only recirculates indoor air — it controls humidity but does nothing for CO₂, PM2.5, or cooking odours. The DBA GEC V series has independent fresh-air and return valves to draw HEPA-filtered outdoor air, plus a built-in 5-in-1 sensor (PM2.5, CO₂, VOC, temperature, humidity) that automatically switches between four modes (dehumidify, fresh-air, mixed, intelligent). One ceiling unit — fresh air × dehumidify × purify × smart sensing.

GEC V 系列與恆溫恆濕 (THC) 系列有何分別？What's the difference between GEC V and THC series?

GEC V (新風淨化天花式抽濕機) 主力解決濕度與室內空氣質素 — 抽濕、新風引入、HEPA 過濾、UV-C 殺菌，需配合現有冷氣使用。THC (恆溫恆濕氣候控制機) 是分體式 (室內機 + 室外機) 全功能 HVAC，可同時製冷、PTC 加熱、抽濕及加濕 — 一機代替「冷氣機 + 抽濕機 + 加濕器」三件組合，適合畫廊、酒窖、博物館及對溫濕度有嚴格要求的高端住宅。 The GEC V (fresh-air ceiling dehumidifier) handles humidity + indoor air quality — dehumidify, fresh-air intake, HEPA, UV-C — and works alongside your existing AC. The THC (climate control unit) is a split-system full HVAC that simultaneously cools, PTC-heats, dehumidifies, and humidifies — one machine replacing the AC + dehumidifier + humidifier stack. THC suits galleries, wine cellars, museums, and premium residences with strict temp/RH requirements.

香港的住宅 / 會所適合哪一款型號？Which model fits a Hong Kong home or clubhouse?

200-400 平方呎一房戶建議 DBA-GEC30V；400-600 平方呎中型單位用 GEC50V；600-800 平方呎建議 GEC70V；800-1,200 平方呎大宅或會所建議 GEC110V。對溫度也有要求的場所 (例如酒窖、畫廊) 可考慮 THC35/50/66 恆溫恆濕系列。所有 V 系列僅 245-285mm 安裝高度，完美隱藏於假天花。提交場地資料後我們會精準建議。 For 200-400 sq ft 1-bedroom flats: DBA-GEC30V. For 400-600 sq ft mid-size units: GEC50V. For 600-800 sq ft: GEC70V. For 800-1,200 sq ft large homes or clubhouses: GEC110V. Spaces needing temperature control too (wine cellars, galleries) should consider THC35/50/66 climate-control units. All V-series fit a 245-285 mm false ceiling. Share your floor plan and we'll size it precisely.

需要管道嗎？安裝麻煩嗎？Do I need ductwork? How complex is installation?

是的，新風天花機需要新風入風管 (室外接管) 及室內出風口配合 — 與安裝中央冷氣機原理相若。我們的工程隊會在裝修期協同進行，假天花完工後機身完全隱藏，只見出風口及回風口格柵。如有現有假天花，亦可施工 — 我們提供免費上門勘察。 Yes — fresh-air ceiling units need a duct from outdoors plus indoor supply grilles, similar to a ducted AC install. Our engineering team coordinates with your renovation to keep the unit hidden in the false ceiling — you only see the supply and return grilles. We can also retrofit existing false ceilings. Free site survey provided.

運作聲音大嗎？會否影響睡眠？How loud is it? Will it affect sleep?

UTC 超薄天花機僅 39-50 dB(A)，比安靜對話更輕。GEC V 新風機運作於 42-49 dB(A)，與電腦風扇相約，臥室全程運作不會影響睡眠。所有機型均可定時開關機並設低速夜間模式。 UTC ultra-thin units run at 39-50 dB(A) — quieter than a hushed conversation. GEC V fresh-air units operate at 42-49 dB(A), comparable to a computer fan. All units offer night-mode low-speed operation and on/off scheduling.

可否與 BMS 樓宇管理系統整合？Can the units integrate with a BMS?

可以。所有 GEC V、UTC、GEC、THC 機型均標配 RS485 (Modbus RTU) 通訊接口，可直接接入樓宇 BMS 進行集中控制、溫濕度監測及故障警報。較小型機型亦支援 WiFi App 遠端控制。 Yes. Every GEC V, UTC, GEC, and THC model includes RS485 with Modbus RTU for direct BMS integration — centralised setpoints, alarms, and runtime telemetry. Smaller models also support WiFi App remote control.

是否可以節省電費？Will it actually save energy?

可以。GEC V 系列採用直流無刷變頻電機，較傳統抽濕機更省電；DBA-UTC20 更獲香港一級能源標籤。乾燥的空氣感覺較涼爽 — 將冷氣設定溫度上調 1-2°C 仍感舒適，節省 8-15% 空調電費。THC 系列 EER 可達 3.22、COP 至 3.2，用一台機代替「冷氣機 + 抽濕機 + 加濕器」三件，實際耗電低於分開運作。 Yes. The GEC V series uses DC brushless inverter motors that beat conventional dehumidifiers on efficiency, and the DBA-UTC20 carries the Hong Kong Grade 1 Energy Label. Drier air feels cooler, so you can raise the AC setpoint 1-2°C and still feel comfortable — typically saving 8-15% on cooling. The THC series achieves EER 3.22 and COP 3.2 by replacing AC + dehumidifier + humidifier with one machine that runs more efficiently than the three separately.
