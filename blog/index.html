排水方案Drainage6 min

冷凝水排放 — 1.8 米內置水泵與重力排放 Drainage — 1.8 m integrated pump and gravity fall

單相機型一律配備 1.8 米內置水泵,先上升至高點再以重力下降。三相機型則以重力排放。排水方式由機型決定,不由項目決定。 Single-phase models all use a 1.8 m integrated pump (rise to a high point, then gravity fall). Three-phase models drain by gravity only. The method is fixed by the unit, not the project.
