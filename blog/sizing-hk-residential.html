容量計算Sizing · 7 分鐘閱讀7 min read

香港住宅與會所的容量配置。 Sizing for HK homes and clubhouses.

「我間屋幾呎,需要邊個型號?」 — 這條問題的答案,從不是只看平方呎。同樣 800 呎,4 人之家配寵物與 1 人獨居的負荷差天共地。 "What model do I need for my flat?" — the answer is never just floor area. The same 800 sq ft with a family of four and pets is a different load from one person living alone.

濕度負荷由哪裡來?

四大來源:

  • 外氣滲入 — 即使密閉式住宅,門窗縫隙仍有 0.2-0.4 ACH(空氣換氣次數)。香港夏季外氣 32°C / 85% RH,內部 25°C / 55% RH,1,000 呎單位每日約 8-15 升
  • 人體呼吸 — 每人每小時釋出 100-150g 水分。四人家庭睡覺 8 小時 = 約 4 升,日常活動再加 3-5 升
  • 日常活動 — 沖涼一次釋出 0.5-1 升;煮飯一次 0.3-0.5 升;曬衫一籃(2-3 公斤濕重)約 1.5-2.5 升
  • 植物與寵物 — 一棵中型 fiddle-leaf fig 每日 0.2 升;一缸金魚每日蒸發 1-3 升

1.5 倍餘量原則

計算出日均負荷後,選擇容量大約 1.5 倍的機型。例如負荷 15 升/日,應選 20-25 升/日容量的機型。原因:

  • 機型不會 24 小時滿負荷運轉 — 較安靜,壽命更長
  • 潮濕季節(2-4 月迴南天)負荷可能突增 50-80%
  • 機型銘牌容量是 30°C / 80% RH 測試值,實際室溫 25°C / 55% RH 環境下,實際抽濕能力會下降 30-40%

特殊情況加碼

  • 地下單位 / 半地下 — 土壤水分滲入,加倍計算
  • 有大型水族缸 — 每缸 1-3 升/日,加大一級
  • 常曬衫 — 每籃加 2 升/日
  • 有皮草、皮具、樂器收藏 — 對 RH 要求較嚴(50-55%),需要更精準的機型,考慮 THC 恆溫恆濕系列

會所及商用考量

會所、健身房、酒店大堂的負荷與住宅不同:

  • 高人流 — 每位活動中的成人每小時釋出 200-300g 水汽(運動時更多)
  • 頻繁開關大門 — 外氣滲入大幅增加
  • 裝飾性綠植 — 高大綠植每株每日 0.5-1 升

香港會所典型負荷:1,500 呎健身房,日間 20 人輪流使用,日均負荷 60-80 升 — 應選 GEC145LD-HP(145 升/日)或兩台 GEC70V 分區。

60 秒選型法

實用面積(呎)× 0.018 = 基線日均濕度負荷(升/日)
每常住人加 1.5 升/日
曬衫、地下單位、水族缸再加 3-5 升/日
總數 × 1.5 = 應選機型容量

Where does moisture load come from?

Four sources:

  • Outside air infiltration — even in sealed flats, doors and windows leak at 0.2–0.4 ACH. HK summer outside at 32°C / 85% RH versus inside at 25°C / 55% RH gives roughly 8–15 L/day for a 1,000 sq ft flat
  • Occupant respiration — each person releases 100–150 g/hour. A family of four sleeping 8 hours ≈ 4 L; another 3–5 L during waking hours
  • Daily activities — one shower releases 0.5–1 L; one cooking session 0.3–0.5 L; a load of damp laundry (2–3 kg wet) drying indoors releases 1.5–2.5 L
  • Plants and pets — a medium fiddle-leaf fig adds 0.2 L/day; a small aquarium evaporates 1–3 L/day

The 1.5× rule

Calculate daily load, then pick a unit rated about 1.5× that. Load 15 L/day → pick a 20–25 L/day unit. Why:

  • The unit avoids running at 100% duty 24/7 — quieter and longer-lived
  • Humid season (Feb–Apr 'wui naam tin') can spike loads 50–80%
  • Nameplate capacity is measured at 30°C / 80% RH; at 25°C / 55% RH real-world conditions, capacity drops 30–40%

Bump up for special cases

  • Ground floor / partial basement — moisture migration through walls, double the calculated load
  • Large aquariums — add a model size per tank
  • Regular indoor laundry drying — add 2 L/day per load
  • Leather, fur, instrument collections — needs tighter RH band (50–55%); consider the THC climate-control range

Clubhouse and commercial

Clubhouse, gym, and hotel-lobby loads differ from residential:

  • High occupancy — each active adult releases 200–300 g/hour (more during exercise)
  • Frequent door cycling — much higher outside air infiltration
  • Decorative plants — large indoor trees add 0.5–1 L per plant per day

Typical HK clubhouse: 1,500 sq ft gym with 20 rotating users daily ≈ 60–80 L/day. Pick a GEC145LD-HP (145 L/day) or two GEC70V split by zone.

The 60-second sizing method

Floor area in sq ft × 0.018 = baseline L/day
Add 1.5 L/day per resident occupant
Add 3–5 L/day for laundry, basement, or aquarium
Multiply by 1.5 to pick the unit
Match to the nearest GEC V capacity above.
